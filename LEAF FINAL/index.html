OUR WORK

India has the largest population of young people in the world today. However, the majority of these youth are left without resources to achieve socio-economic success. The fight against poverty begins at school. Enabling children to complete secondary education, empowering them with life skills like teamwork, problem-solving, communication, and making them more resilient to the fallouts of poverty, will set in motion the process to undo poverty.

VISION

We envision a world in which marginalized young people are provided the opportunities to emerge from poverty to lead fulfilling lives that positively contribute to their communities and future generation

MISSION

As vulnerable young people transition to adulthood, we aim to equip them with the necessary skills for a sustainable livelihood and promising future. Facilitate learning and coordination between organisations working for children and their future

CULTURE

Our culture is centred around the pillars of excellence, trust, dedication and transparency. We believe in open communication and positivity, with the goal of delivering results for our partners. Our culture is one of partnership, diversity and ensuring employee satisfaction.

Childhood to Livelihood

This approach is focused on breaking the cycle of poverty by empowering at-risk children with the resources to complete secondary education and gain skills to become employable upon graduation. The program provides positive reinforcement of life skills especially for girls. Educating girls serves to have them continuing to college, marrying much later, having fewer children and most importantly educating their own children. What usually takes 4 generations, completion of a high school education for boys and girls, serves as a critical intervention for breaking the cycle of poverty or the entire family
